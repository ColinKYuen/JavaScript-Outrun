OutRun in JavaScript

How to Play

Drive through the city and try and reach the farthest you can without crashing!

Controls

  • Press ENTER to Begin/Select
  • Press W to Accelerate
  • Press S to Brake
  • Press A / D to turn
  • Press ESC to pause
  • Press Q / E to switch Tracks

Resolutions

You can change the resoultions within the Settings Menu, found from the Main Menu.
